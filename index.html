Welcome to Holten Wellness

At Holten Wellness Center, we are your one-stop shop for comprehensive wellness in Dayton, OH. We are dedicated to providing you with the highest quality care and a personalized approach to your health. Our team of experts is here to help you achieve your wellness goals, whether you are recovering from an auto, work, or sports injury, or simply seeking to improve your overall health.

We believe in a holistic approach to health and wellness, and we offer a variety of services to help you achieve your health goals. Our team of experienced professionals is here to help you every step of the way. We are committed to providing you with the best possible care and helping you live a healthier, happier life.

Our Integrated Services

Chiropractic Care

Our experienced chiropractors provide adjustments to help you with back pain, neck pain, and other issues.

Massage Therapy

Our licensed massage therapists provide a variety of massage techniques to help you relax and recover.

Nutrition & Fitness

We offer personalized nutrition and fitness plans to help you achieve your health and wellness goals.

Rehabilitation

We offer personalized rehabilitation programs to help you regain strength and mobility after an injury.

Disc Decompression

We offer non-surgical spinal decompression to help with herniated discs, sciatica, and other spinal issues.

Shockwave Therapy

A non-invasive treatment that can help with a variety of musculoskeletal conditions.
